    Shadow Black Obituary, Death – It is with heavy hearts that we received the news of Shadow Black’s passing, marking a poignant loss for Trellis HIV & Community Care. His steadfast support and dedication deeply impacted numerous lives within our community. Today, December 24, 2023, we mourn the departure of a beloved friend, volunteer, and cherished member of our community.

    Shadow was more than a volunteer; he was a beacon of warmth and solace. His stories and infectious smiles served as both entertainment and solace, providing comfort to those in need. His unwavering commitment to our cause touched many, leaving a lasting imprint on the hearts of those he encountered.

    His kindness and boundless compassion were integral parts of his character, traits that endeared him to everyone fortunate enough to know him. While his physical presence may be absent, Shadow’s spirit and humor remain alive in the memories and lives he touched.

    The loss of Shadow Black leaves a void within our community that words cannot express. However, we find solace in the enduring impact he made and the legacy of kindness he leaves behind. His contributions will forever be remembered, serving as a reminder of the power of compassion and the influence of a genuine, caring spirit.

    Though he may no longer be with us, Shadow’s legacy of kindness and empathy will continue to resonate within Trellis HIV & Community Care and beyond. His absence will be felt deeply, but the memories of his warmth and laughter will remain a cherished part of our community’s tapestry. Rest in peace, Shadow, your light will forever shine in the lives you touched.
