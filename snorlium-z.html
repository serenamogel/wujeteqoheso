    The Snorlium Z (Japanese: カビゴンＺ Kabigon Z) is a type of Z-Crystal introduced in Pokémon Sun and Moon. It is a Pokémon-specific Z-Crystal associated with Snorlax and allows it to upgrade its Giga Impact into the special Z-Move Pulverizing Pancake.

    Effect

    Bag item

    Can create Snorlium Z held items for the player's Pokémon.

    Held item

    In battle

    Allows Snorlax (or a Pokémon transformed into Snorlax) to upgrade its Giga Impact into the special Z-Move Pulverizing Pancake.

    Unaffected by item negating effects (such as Embargo and Klutz). Item manipulating effects (such as Trick and Fling) cannot remove a Z-Crystal from a Pokémon.

    Rayquaza cannot Mega Evolve if it holds a Z-Crystal.

    Outside of battle

    A held Snorlium Z is automatically removed if the Pokémon is traded between players.
