    ➡ Hira Mani Biography

    Hira Mani is a household name in the Pakistani entertainment industry as she has continuously been giving amazing performances in many television serials. She is among the ones who got much popularity for not just her acting talent, yet elegant looks as well. She is popularly known for appearing in the most loved serial ‘Do Bol’ in which she shared the screen space alongside Affan Waheed. Also, she did many projects alongside Junaid Khan who is also a great, popular actor. However, this does not end here as we have compiled some unknown things you probably did not know about.

    8 Things You Didn’t Know About Hira Mani

    1- Want to be in the Limelight

    Hira Mani always loves to be in the limelight as she loves to perform and still can’t believe that she has achieved what she dreamt of.

    2- Got engaged first at the age of 17

    Hira Mani often reveals that she got engaged for the first time at the age of 17. Later, she called off the engagement and got married to Salman Saqib Shaikh aka Mani.

    3- Credits her Husband

    Hira Mani credits her husband for being a strong support system throughout her career as he never stops her to pursue her dreams.

    4- She & Social Media

    Hira Mani is an avid social media user as she loves sharing her life updates, photos, and videos with her fans and followers.

    5- Dark Phase

    Hira Mani suffered from anxiety and depression after joining the field of showbiz. She always calls it a dark phase of her life.

    6- Her husband is a lot like her father

    She thinks that her husband Mani is a lot like her father because he loves and takes care of her as her father does.

    7- Afraid of dying

    Hira Mani is afraid of dying as she loves living her life to the fullest. She believes that life is so beautiful and full of colors and adventures.

    8- Family Girl

    Hira Mani is a family girl as she loves spending quality time with her husband and kids. They often go out for spending a great time together at restaurants and other spots.
