Route 5 (Japanese: ５番道路 Route 5) is a route located in northern Kanto. It connects Cerulean City in the north to Saffron City in the south. The northern terminus of the Underground Path is located on this route. Route description Route 5 is a small pathway that is divided into three vertical parts: two pathways and a one-way, grass-filled middle with ledges that separates the two two-way paths. This middle section, accessible only from the Cerulean end, leads to the Pokémon Day Care just north of Saffron.
