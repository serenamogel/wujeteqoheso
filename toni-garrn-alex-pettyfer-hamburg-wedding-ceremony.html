    A wedding bell tolls to a German Fashion model and actress Toni Garrn and her beau, Alex Pettyfer. The lovebirds, who have been in a relationship for a few years, have tied their nuptial knot during Germany's weekend in Hamburg. The 28 years old model opened about her marriage to Pettyfer through an Instagram post on 4 October 2020.

    The lovebirds showcased their wedding band rings as they kissed while snapping the picture. 'Now you really get to call me wifey ❤️,' she wrote on her official Instagram account. 

    Similarly, Alex Pettyfer shared the same image on his Instagram and wrote, 'Mr. and Mrs. Pettyfer.'

    As for the wedding dress, Toni Garrn donned in a cream gown with a straw hat tied to her neck with a black ribbon. Her newlywed husband, Pettyfer, accompanied her in a grey checked wedding tuxedo at the time.

    On 4 October 2020, the newlywed lovebirds teased their wedding ceremony on their Instagram stories. They featured a picture of Toni Garrn topping her shoestring-strap dress with a straw hat. 'Too summery for 10C? Naaaa,' she captioned.

    Toni Garrn and Alex Pettyfer (©: US Magazine)

    The happy news of Garrn getting married to Pettyfer came after ten months after their engagement. Alex Pettyfer proposed his love interest back on Christmas Eve 2019. They waited nearly a week to announce their engagement and shared the news on New Year's Eve 2020.

    Alex posted a kissing Gif on his Instagram on 31 December 2019 and wrote, 'Some people marry their best friend. Some people marry their soulmates. I got both.'

    Likewise, Garrn shared the same post on her Instagram and mentioned, 'Christmas Eve, the love of my life surprised me on his knees, asking me to be his forever. He changed my life the day we met and showed me what love really is – so yes, I cannot wait to spend every day (well almost) of my life with you @alexpettyfer.'

    Both the stars have a different romantic background, where they dated numerous partners, respectively. Toni Garrn, 28, was in a relationship with Leonardo DiCaprio from 2013 to 2014. Similarly, she was rumored to leading a romantic excursion with the NBA player, Chandler Parsons, in 2015.

    On the Alex Pettyfer's side, he dated Glee actress Diana Agron in 2011. He dated and later got engaged to Riley Keough in 2012; however, their bond did not last long.

    Similarly, he was in a romantic bond with a model named Marloes Horst for a couple of years from 2014 to 2016.
