    Unexpected Death of Paula Troutman

    The sudden and untimely passing of Paula Troutman has left a void in our school community that will be difficult to fill. Paula was not only a dedicated educator but also a compassionate mentor and leader. Her unwavering commitment to the students and staff of Fredonia Middle School will always be remembered.

    Announcement by Fredonia Central School District

    The Fredonia Central School District is deeply saddened to announce the unexpected death of our esteemed Middle School Principal, Paula Troutman. In a statement shared on Facebook, the district expressed its condolences and assured the community that counseling and bereavement support services would be made available to those in need.

The team will be available at the High School Library on January 2nd, allowing everyone to come together, share their grief, and find solace in one another’s presence.

    In the wake of the unexpected passing of Paula Troutman, the Fredonia Central School District is committed to providing a compassionate and supportive environment for the entire school community.     We recognize that the faculty and staff of Fredonia Middle School are deeply affected by the loss of their esteemed colleague and leader, Paula Troutman. To assist them in processing their emotions and finding solace, a dedicated crisis team will be available at the High School Library. This team will provide a safe space for faculty and staff to come together, share their thoughts and feelings, and receive the support they need to navigate this challenging time.

    We understand that the loss of Paula Troutman has had a profound impact on the students and families of Fredonia Middle School.     We encourage everyone to take advantage of these support services and come together as we navigate this difficult journey of grief and loss. The Fredonia Central School District is committed to providing a nurturing and caring environment for all members of our community during this challenging time.
