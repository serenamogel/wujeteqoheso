    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Taraji P. Henson was born and raised in Washington, D.C. She attended Howard University, where she studied drama. Henson moved to Los Angeles in 1996 to pursue her acting career and landed her first professional acting gig on the television show, “Smart Guy”.

    Rise to Stardom

    Henson’s breakthrough came in 2001 when she starred in the critically acclaimed film, “Baby Boy”. Her performance in the film earned her widespread recognition and opened doors for more significant roles in both television and film.

    Television Success

    Henson’s television career took off when she landed a role in the CBS drama, “Person of Interest”. However, her most notable television role is that of Cookie Lyon in the Fox drama series, “Empire”, for which she won a Golden Globe Award.

    Film Success

    On the big screen, Henson has starred in several successful films, including “Hustle & Flow”, “The Curious Case of Benjamin Button”, for which she received an Academy Award nomination, and “Hidden Figures”.

    Net Worth and Earnings

    As of 2024, Taraji P. Henson’s net worth is estimated to be around $30 million. Her wealth primarily comes from her acting career, both in television and film. She reportedly earned $175,000 per episode for her role in “Empire”.

    Endorsements and Other Ventures

    Besides acting, Henson also earns from endorsements and other business ventures. She has endorsement deals with several brands, including MAC Cosmetics and Target. She also has a line of hair care products, TPH by Taraji.

    Real Estate Investments

    Henson has invested in real estate over the years. She owns a $6.4 million mansion in Los Angeles and a $1.4 million condo in Chicago.

    Philanthropy

    Henson is also known for her philanthropic efforts. She founded the Boris Lawrence Henson Foundation, named after her late father, to raise awareness about mental health issues in the African American community.

    Future Projects and Earnings

    Looking ahead, Henson’s net worth is expected to continue to grow. She has several upcoming projects, including a spin-off of “Empire” centered on her character, Cookie Lyon. She is also set to star in the film “The Color Purple” and the television series “Annie Live!”.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, Taraji P. Henson’s net worth in 2024 is projected to be around $30 million. Her wealth comes from her successful acting career, endorsements, and other business ventures. Despite her success, Henson remains grounded and is known for her philanthropic efforts. With several upcoming projects in the pipeline, her net worth is expected to continue to grow in the coming years.
