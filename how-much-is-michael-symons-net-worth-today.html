    Michael Symon is an American chef, restaurateur, television personality, and author. who has a net worth of US$6 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. The professional chef makes his money from the sale of his cook books and cookware line as well as brand endorsement deals.

    Who is Michael Symon?

    Michael D. Symon was born on September 19, 1969 in Cleveland, Ohio in the United States of America. He was raised in North Olmsted, Ohio where he attended St. Richard School. After his junior years, he went to St. Edward High School in Lakewood, where he graduated in 1987.

    Upon completion from high school, Symon took up a part-time job as a chef at Geppetto’s Pizza and Ribs on Warren Rd. He then enrolled to the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park, New York to perfect his culinary skills.

    After his education, he traveled to Europe to work and explore in various restaurants to hone his culinary skills. Symon spent two years in the Netherlands, learning the language and working in a Michelin-starred restaurant and also worked in France for a period.

    How many houses and cars does Michael Symon have?

    Michael Symon owns several houses and properties across the United States. Among these homes include a US$1.15 million Cleveland Heights, a US$909,000 North Ridgeville, Ohio and another US$1.6 million Mediterranean-style home in Palm Beach. The chef owns a number of cars as well.
