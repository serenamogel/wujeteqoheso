    In case you haven’t heard, Olivia Rodrigo is touring this year. The singer-songwriter first shared exciting news about her GUTS World Tour back in September on her social media platforms, and we've been hardly able to control our excitement ever since. Get ready to snag those tickets now while there’s still time and catch her live as Rolling Stone hails her “GUTS” album as a perfect instant classic (and we can't help but agree!).

    The 2024 Guts tour spans 77 shows across North America and Europe this year. It kicks off on February 23 in Palm Springs, California, and wraps up on August 17 in Los Angeles, California.

    Olivia will be joined by special guests The Breeders, PinkPantheress, Chappell Roan, and Remi Wolf, who will open.

    What You Can Expect to Pay for the Olivia Rodrigo GUTS Concert

    Ticket prices have been fluctuating on resale sites. But yes, you missed out on pre-sale and regular sale when ticket sales first kicked off. Original tickets (that ranged from $40 to $200) through Ticketmaster are no longer available, having sold out quickly during the presale events back in September of last year.

    Some resellers seem to be dropping prices, especially in the U.S. We actually spotted some under $200 in certain cities (like Dallas, Texas, and Omaha, Nebraska). However, popular spots like New York and California are still pricey, hovering around $340 at the lowest. On the bright side, European tickets can be found for as low as $40 in Oslo or $70 in Paris.

    Want a premium experience? VIP packages and “best seats” options range from $1,000 to $4,000+.

    The best and most legitimate third-party verified resale platforms you should consider purchasing tickets from are StubHub, SeatGeek, and Vivid Seats.

    Special Silver Star Tickets Availability

    Don’t have a big budget? Rodrigo is supposedly making Silver Star tickets available at a later date, which are rumored to be priced at $20 (plus taxes and fees). You would have had to register for the tickets ahead of time through Ticketmaster. The deadline to register was February 13, and those who are lucky enough to be chosen will receive an invitation to buy tickets on February 16. "Only registered fans with approved links will be able to join," states the registration site.

    The number of these tickets is undisclosed, and they will be limited to two per purchase. And, you will be buying blind as seat locations for Silver Star tickets will only be revealed upon picking them up from the box office on the concert day.

    GUTS Tour Setlist Details

    Unfortunately, we don’t know yet! As soon as the first show date kicks off, don’t forget to check Setlist.fm to get a full rundown of the songs Rodrigo includes in the show. We’re hoping all her hits from her album “Sour” (in addition to “GUTS”) will get some airtime, but you never know.

    But for sure, you can expect to scream along to “GUTS” hit songs “Vampire” and “Get Him Back!” with Rodrigo and fellow fans.

    How to Pick the Right Concert Tickets For You

    Time to use those filters, Olivia Rodrigo fans! As you search for seats on ticket sale sites, you'll be able to toggle based on preferences like sections and prices.

    As you select tickets, remember to take note of where the seats are in relation to the stage. To arm yourself with success, you can use AViewFromMySeat.com, which gives you user-generated photos of the venues from previous visits.

    We hope all of this helps and you get to see Rodrigo in action in 2024!
